About Savvy TechPacks

Site Goal

SavvyTechPacks.com provides apparel entrepreneurs and emerging clothing brands with access to professionally developed, production-proven tech packs that can be purchased and downloaded instantly as a foundation for launching products.

Who This Is For

This site is designed for emerging apparel entrepreneurs, startup clothing brands, independent designers, and small businesses who have strong creative ideas but need reliable documentation to communicate clearly with manufacturers.

Why It Helps

You get a faster starting point for development • fewer costly errors • and clearer communication with factories. Use these packs as a base and customize as needed. Speed matters, but accuracy matters even more.

Many founders are working with limited budgets and timelines, so ready-made tech packs can be a practical way to move from idea to production with more confidence.

What You’ll Find Inside a Tech Pack

  • Construction details and callouts
  • Measurement specs and tolerances
  • Materials and trim information

How It Works

  1. Choose a tech pack that matches your product category.
  2. Download and review the pages.
  3. Update brand details, sizing, and materials before sending to a factory.

Quick Glossary

Tech Pack
A complete document that communicates how a garment is made, measured, and finished.
Spec Sheet
A page that lists measurements, points of measure, and tolerances for sizing consistency.
Bill of Materials (BOM)
A list of fabrics, trims, labels, and components needed to produce the garment.
