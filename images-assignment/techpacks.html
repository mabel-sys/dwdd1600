Tech Pack Categories

Menswear

Technical documentation designed for men's apparel, including tops, bottoms, and outerwear. These tech packs focus on fit, durability, and construction standards commonly used in men’s production.

Womenswear

Tech packs created for women's apparel, with attention to fit, proportion, and garment detailing. Categories may include dresses, blouses, skirts, and tailored styles.

Children's Wear

Apparel documentation developed for children's clothing, prioritizing comfort, safety, and scalable sizing for growing bodies.
